Lasagna

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish made of wide, flat lasagna noodles layered with sauce, cheese, and other fillings like meat or vegetables.
It is a popular Italian dish with many regional variations, but common versions include a meat sauce or ragù, béchamel sauce, and
ricotta and mozzarella cheeses.

Pico De Gallo Salsa

Pico de gallo is a fresh, chunky salsa made with diced tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and jalape単o or serrano peppers, seasoned with salt and lime juice.
It's also known as salsa fresca or salsa cruda because it is not cooked or blended, relying on the fresh, chopped ingredients for its texture and flavor.

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • jalape単o
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper

Ingridients

  • 6 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, minced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 jalape単o pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch ground cumin, or to taste
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingridients
  2. Place tomatoes, onions, cilantro, jalape単o pepper, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Stir wll until combined
  4. Enjoy!
